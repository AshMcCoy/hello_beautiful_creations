Our Story

Thank you for visiting my shop. My name is Toni and I am a stay at home mom of 3 beautiful little blessings. I make everything in my shop by hand. I started this shop to help supplement my families income and because I love crafting. DIY projects and crafting are my relaxation time. I try my best to keep this website up to date. I like to create new things, so when I run out of an item, it usually does not return unless there is a high demand for it. I absolutely love creating things and serving others. If there is a problem with any of your orders, I will do my best to accommodate. Customer service is my top priority. I love what I do and I am so incredibly thankful that you are here. I know there are MANY different places you could shop, so I am so grateful for your support.
